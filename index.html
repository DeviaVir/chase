a little about me

My name is Chase Sillevis, I have been a web developer for a couple of years now and have been loving it so far. I'm very passionate about what I do.
You will find that I am flexible and willing to add value. I live in the Netherlands, 22 years old, am not a 9-5 kind of person and when it comes to CSS and HTML I really know what I'm doing. I have the insatiable desire to push the limits in terms of imagination and what can be made a reality on the web.

  • i do

    Nearly everything on the web, or around it. I manage linux webservers, develop backends for small or huge projects, build up awesome, responsive frontends or set up a new multi-node wireless network.

  • ideals

    Stay hungry, stay foolish. I want to keep learning new things, and improve things I already master. I am blessed with an eagerness to learn and to correct myself, without being arrogant about it.

  • my skills

    I master HTML (with HAML or plain), CSS (with SASS, LESS, Stylus or plain). I have experience with PHP and MySQL. Played around with Ruby and Python. Know my way around Photoshop and Illustrator.
